How to Play

  • 1. The Goal Find the hidden atoms inside the box by shooting rays from the numbered ports on the edge.
  • 2. Firing Rays Click a number to fire a ray. Observe the result in the Game Log. There are 3 possible outcomes:
    • Exited: The ray passed through and exited at a different port.
    • Hit / Absorbed: The ray hit an atom head-on.
    • Reflected: The ray exited from the same port it entered.
  • 3. Ray Behavior
    • A ray is deflected 90Â° when it passes a cell diagonally adjacent to an atom.
    • A ray is reflected (U-Turn) if it passes a cell diagonally adjacent to two atoms.
    • A ray entering right next to an atom on the edge is also reflected.
  • 4. Guessing Click any cell in the grid to place a guess marker (a red circle). Click again to remove it. When you've placed all your guesses, click "Reveal Answer" to check your score.
